Content Strategy

This single page site will present the following content:

Welcome

This is the 1st content section of the page where I will introduce myself and my experience and interests as a Front End Web Developer.

My Story

In this section I ill give a brief overview of my experience in Web Development. I will expand on my history and passion that led me to this field. I will discuss specific key skills and strengths to let potential clients know what I bring to the table and how I can best fit into their projects.

My Work

In this section I will display an image gallery showcasing several projects I have worked on. For each project I will have a few screenshots and brief description of the projects goals, obstacles and solutions discovered in the process.

Contact me

In this section I will have a simple contact form that has entry full name, email address and short description, I will also have a list of my Email address, phone number, LinkedIn, and my github.
