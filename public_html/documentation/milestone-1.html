PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose

The website of this project is a personal portfolio of my professional web development experience.

Audience

the audience category this website targeting is future employers including: HR managers, recruiters.

Goal

The main goal of this website is for users to get in contact with me regarding employment offers. And the the other goal is to show my previous work achievements and progress.

Persona

Name

Sammy Sosa

Job Title: CEO construction company

Sammy Sosa has been running a humble construction company for 5 years in Fort Lauderdale area. He's been looking to expand projects to the greater South Florida, but he's short on sells staff and feels the need for a website to expand his presence.

Frustration

Sammy has been focused on construction and property management since he started the company but he lacks knowledge in modern web development. He is also a very busy person and doesn't have time to research his market for the web.

Goal

Sammy wants to expand the exposure of his company's services to the greater South Florida region and he believes an online presence built with modern web practices can help him meet that goal. The research that he and his team have done for web services in the area has lead them to the conclusion that Massimo Bouchentouf Web Services LLC is the best in the area.

User Story

As a user, I need to decide if Simo Bouchentouf meets the the requirements needed for my goal.
